About the author

This could be a section on a new page with the biography of the author. A printed book has this text often at the back of the cover. In an e-book this is not possible. Sometimes the publisher chooses to show it at the very end of the e-book. I think it is more logical to put it in the frontmatter.

Because in this case the image of the author does not matter, I have put the image of his dog instead. Is better looking too.

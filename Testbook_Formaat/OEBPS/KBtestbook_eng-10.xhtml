Chapter 1 - Character styles

Texts in an ebook can be divided roughly in paragraphs and characters. Paragraphs always have a tag, a ‘style’. Often that is a p-tag, meaning paragraph. It can also be a header, marked by a h-tag, meaning header.

Withing text blocks or paragraphs individual words or characters can also be tagged with inline character styles.

The text blocks in this chapter are tagged with standard paragraphs styles. Here we will look into the inline character styles. In general, inline character styles mean an exception, like a temporary change of font or weight. Bold, italic, bold-italic, small caps... that kind of things.

The individual character style does not specify the white spacing, line height or margins. That is covered iwwn the block styles.

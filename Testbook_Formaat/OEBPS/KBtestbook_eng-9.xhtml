Part 1

The first part of this book contains three chapters. Next there will be two parts. In the table of contents, there are two levels. The chapters in each part are level two.
Sections in the book like introduction, preface, epilogue, register, but also the colophon, ‘about the author’, and Table of Contents, are parts that can have the same prominent headers as the chapters. However, these will show at the top level and not under a ‘Part’.
